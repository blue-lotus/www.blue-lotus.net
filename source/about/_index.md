Blue-lotus is a security research team in Tsinghua University. We have played several famous [Capture The Flag](http://ctftime.org/ctf-wtf/) games around the world([Defcon](https://www.defcon.org/), [Hack.LU](http://2012.hack.lu/), [iCTF](http://ictf.cs.ucsb.edu/), [RuCTFE](http://ructf.org/e/2012/)) in the last few years under the team name of blue-lotus. You can find our rankings Â in [CTFtime](http://ctftime.org/team/1941/).

We also have some other players from other universities and teams.
